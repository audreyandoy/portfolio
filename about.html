About Audrey

I am a Seattle-based Web developer with a growing passion in front-end development and data visualizations. Working previously as an implementation consultant for municipal financial software, I learned the importance of creating interactive applications to improve business processes for the benefit of all end-users. I work best in collaborative environments with fellow creative and independent minds. When my head isn't in my laptop you can find me baking something sugary or outside enjoying a hike.
